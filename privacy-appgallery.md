This Privacy Policy explains what data FPL Helper processes in the Huawei AppGallery release.

## 1) Short summary
- No user account or login is required.
- Flight plan content you enter is stored locally on your device.
- This release does not sell personal data.
- This release does not use advertising SDKs.

## 2) Data we process
### 2.1 Data you enter in the app
- Flight plan fields (for example ICAO items 7–19, templates, drafts, notes).
- These data are stored locally on your device storage.
- We do not upload your flight plan content to our own servers.

### 2.2 Technical data
- For this Huawei free release, no account profile data is collected by the app itself.
- If diagnostics/crash reporting are enabled in a future update, this Privacy Policy will be updated before release.

## 3) Why we process data
We process local app data only to provide core app functions:
- create, edit, import, export, and view flight plans
- save drafts/templates on device
- generate ATS/SAR output and PDF export

## 4) Sharing and third parties
- We do not sell user data.
- We do not share flight plan content with third parties.
- If a future version adds third-party diagnostics or payment services, they will be listed here before publication.

## 5) Retention
- App content remains on your device until you delete it or uninstall the app.
- Exported files remain where you save/share them.
